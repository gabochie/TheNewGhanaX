  <h1 class="manifesto-title">10 Traps Keeping Ghana Stuck</h1>
  
  <p class="manifesto-lead">
    Ghana isn't just held back by external factors — it's shackled by <strong>internal psychological traps</strong>. 
    These mental prisons keep our brightest minds small, our systems broken, and our nation stuck in first gear.
  </p>
  
  <p class="manifesto-subtitle">
    This isn't about blame. It's about <strong>awareness</strong>. Because once you see the trap, you can step out of it.
  </p>
</div>

<!-- The 10 Traps Grid -->
<section class="traps-section" aria-labelledby="traps-heading">
  <h2 id="traps-heading" class="section-title">The 10 Systemic Traps</h2>
  
  <div class="traps-grid">
    <a href="/traps/survival-mindset/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">01</span>
      <h3>Survival Mindset</h3>
      <p>Trained to focus on daily survival, not legacy or innovation</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/authority-worship/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">02</span>
      <h3>Authority Worship</h3>
      <p>Once someone is in power, they're untouchable</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/scarcity-mentality/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">03</span>
      <h3>Scarcity Mentality</h3>
      <p>The false belief that wealth and opportunity are limited</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/colonial-logic/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">04</span>
      <h3>Colonial Logic</h3>
      <p>Deep programming that foreign = better</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/short-termism/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">05</span>
      <h3>Short-Termism</h3>
      <p>Everything is about quick gain — "sakawa planning"</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/over-spiritualization/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">06</span>
      <h3>Over-Spiritualization</h3>
      <p>Praying for what requires planning</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/inferiority-complex/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">07</span>
      <h3>Inferiority Complex</h3>
      <p>Shrinking potential to avoid being seen as proud</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/dependency-programming/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">08</span>
      <h3>Dependency Programming</h3>
      <p>Belief that salvation must come from "above"</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/status-symbols/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">09</span>
      <h3>Status Symbol Syndrome</h3>
      <p>Prioritizing image over impact</p>
    </a>
    
    <a href="/traps/not-that-deep/" class="trap-tile">
      <span class="trap-num">10</span>
      <h3>The "Not That Deep" Delusion</h3>
      <p>Avoiding serious issues with jokes and apathy</p>
    </a>
  </div>
</section>
