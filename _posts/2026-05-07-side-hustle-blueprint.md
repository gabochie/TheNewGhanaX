## The ₵3,000/Month Side Hustle Framework

I'm going to give you the exact 90-day blueprint.

**Goal:** ₵3,000/month recurring income.
**Method:** Service-based (fastest to cash)
**Model:** Retainer clients (predictable income)

### Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-10)

**Day 1-2: Choose Your Service**

Pick ONE thing you can deliver confidently:
- Social media management
- Content writing
- Virtual assistance
- Basic graphic design
- Customer service/admin support

**Don't overthink. You can change later.**

**Day 3-5: Package Your Offer**

Create 3 service tiers:

**Basic (₵1,500/month):**
- What: [Service] for 10 hours/month
- Who: Small businesses, solopreneurs
- Deliverable: [Specific outcome]

**Standard (₵3,000/month):**
- What: [Service] for 20 hours/month  
- Who: Growing businesses
- Deliverable: [Better outcome + reporting]

**Premium (₵5,000/month):**
- What: [Service] for 30 hours/month + strategy
- Who: Established businesses
- Deliverable: [Full solution with results guarantee]

**Example for Social Media Management:**
- Basic: 12 posts/month, 1 platform, basic scheduling
- Standard: 20 posts/month, 2 platforms, engagement management, monthly report
- Premium: 30 posts/month, 3 platforms, community management, growth strategy, weekly calls

**Day 6-8: Build Proof**

You need samples even if you've never had a client.

**Create:**
- 3 mock projects (do the work for fictional businesses)
- 1 free project for a real small business (friend, family, local shop)
- Document everything with screenshots

**Day 9-10: Set Up Systems**

**Tools (all free or low-cost):**
- Portfolio: Notion page or simple website (Google Sites)
- Invoicing: Wave or Excel templates
- Communication: WhatsApp Business + email
- Scheduling: Google Calendar
- Deliverables: Google Drive folders

**Total startup cost: Under ₵200**

### Phase 2: First Client Sprint (Days 11-30)

**Goal:** Land 1 client at ₵1,500-3,000/month

**Day 11-15: Network Mining**

List 50 people who:
- Own businesses
- Manage businesses
- Know business owners
- Have connections

Sources:
- Phone contacts
- LinkedIn connections
- Church/community groups
- School alumni
- Twitter/X followers

**Day 16-20: The Offer**

Reach out with this script:

> "Hi [Name], hope you're doing well. I'm offering [service] for small businesses to help them [specific outcome]. I know you're connected to [industry], and I wondered if you know any business owners who might need this? I'm offering the first month at 50% off to build my portfolio."

**Send 10 messages daily = 50 messages in 5 days**

Expected response rate: 10-20% = 5-10 conversations

**Day 21-25: Discovery Calls**

When someone shows interest:

1. **Discovery call (15 min):**
   - "Tell me about your business"
   - "What are your challenges with [problem you solve]?"
   - "What would solving this mean for you?"
   - "Have you tried solving this before?"

2. **Present solution:**
   - "Based on what you shared, I recommend [package]. This would include [deliverables] and should achieve [outcome]."

3. **Price anchor:**
   - "My standard rate for this is ₵X, but since you're one of my first clients and I want to prove myself, I can do ₵Y for the first 2 months."

4. **Close or follow up:**
   - "Does this make sense for where you are right now?"
   - If no: "What would need to change for this to work?"
   - If yes: "Great! I'll send the agreement tonight. We can start Monday."

**Day 26-30: Close First Deal**

Follow up on calls:
- Send proposal/agreement within 24 hours
- Include: Scope, deliverables, timeline, price, payment terms
- Offer payment plan if needed (50% upfront, 50% on delivery)

**Target:** 1 client at ₵1,500-3,000 by day 30.

**If you don't close:** Iterate on your offer, pricing, or messaging. Don't quit.

### Phase 3: Scale to ₵3,000 (Days 31-60)

**Goal:** 2-3 clients totaling ₵3,000+/month

**Day 31-40: Deliver Excellence**

Your first client determines everything:
- Over-deliver on every promise
- Communicate proactively (daily updates)
- Ask for feedback weekly
- Document results

**Ask for testimonial at day 30:**
> "You've been a great client to work with. Would you mind sharing a quick testimonial about your experience? This helps me help more businesses like yours."

**Day 41-50: Systematize**

Create templates and SOPs:
- Onboarding checklist
- Weekly report template
- Content calendar template
- FAQ responses

This lets you serve more clients without more time.

**Day 51-60: Land Client #2**

Use your first client as social proof:
> "I just helped [Client] achieve [result] with [service]. Looking for 1 more business to help this month. Know anyone who needs [outcome]?"

Close second client at full price (no discount needed now that you have proof).

**Month 2 Target:** 2 clients at ₵2,500-3,000 total.

### Phase 4: Stabilize (Days 61-90)

**Goal:** Consistent ₵3,000/month with systems

**Day 61-75: Add Client #3 or Raise Prices**

Option A: Take on 3rd smaller client (₵1,500) = ₵4,500 total
Option B: Raise prices for new clients (25% increase)

**Day 76-90: Build Recurring Revenue**

Convert project work to retainers:
- "You've seen results from the one-time project. Want to keep this going monthly so you don't lose momentum?"

Retainers = predictable income = you can plan your life.

**Month 3 Target:** ₵3,000-5,000/month recurring.

## The Daily Schedule (While Working Full-Time)

**Weekday:**
- 5:00-7:00 AM: Deep work (client delivery, proposals)
- 9:00 AM-5:00 PM: Day job
- 12:00-1:00 PM: Lunch + admin (emails, messages)
- 7:00-9:00 PM: Client calls, networking, learning

**Weekend:**
- Saturday: 4 hours (batch content, strategy, catch-up)
- Sunday: Off (rest is production)

**Total: 18-20 hours/week**

**Non-negotiables:**
- No day job tasks during hustle time
- No hustle during rest time
- One full day off weekly
- Sleep 7 hours minimum

## Avoiding the Common Traps

### Trap 1: Perfectionism
> "I need to learn more before I start"

**No.** You learn by doing. Start messy. Fix as you go.

### Trap 2: Shiny Object Syndrome
> "Maybe I should try [other business] instead"

**Pick one.** Give it 90 days. Most people fail because they quit at day 45.

### Trap 3: Underpricing
> "I can't charge that much, I'm new"

**Your price signals your value.** Start at market rate, discount for proof of concept only.

### Trap 4: Scope Creep
Client asks for extras not in the agreement.

**Response:** "I'd love to help with that. It's outside our current scope, but I can add it for ₵X or we can discuss upgrading your package."

### Trap 5: Burnout
Working every waking hour.

**This kills the hustle.** Schedule rest. Take days off. Protect your energy.
